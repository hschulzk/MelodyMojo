Early Childhood Music & Movement

Every child loves to sing, dance and play! We do too! Each week, our students develop rhythm, balance and other skills so important to developing language, math skills, balance, coordinate, social skills, critical thinking, organization skills, creativity and confidence. All that in an hour of song, dance, laughing, running, playing, puppets, super-cool instruments and snuggling and bonding. It’s too much fun!

Kid-tested and loved since 1983, internationally renowned music educator Lynn Kleiner’s Music Rhapsody fosters the love of music in an exciting, creative program that, to its core, acknowledges and values the input and creativity of every child.

Classes held at Steps Dance Center, 1025 Rose Creek Drive, Suite 460, Woodstock, GA, 30189

Simply Music Piano

You love music. Maybe you’d like to play piano—it's incredibly cool! Are you looking for a way to keep your brain young? Or do want your child to find joy and success in creating and performing. My students, ages 6 to 90, start playing all kinds of music starting at their first lesson. They have a blast with the Simply Music Piano method in class and playing at home—or anywhere. It’s even more fun in our Piano with Friends classes. Every lesson is in a highly self-affirming, success-oriented environment.

Students needs an acoustic piano or a weighted 61+ keys, keyboard with a pedal at home in order to practice (for example, Casio Privia series).

Lessons are currently offered in Woodstock, Marietta, and Kennesaw.

Everyone is musical—including you! Prove it to yourself.
